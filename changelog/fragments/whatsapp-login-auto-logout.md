- WhatsApp/Login: auto-logout stored credentials after a failed `web.login.wait` (including exhausted 515 restarts) so users can retry linking immediately without manually clicking Logout on the dashboard. Enriched the success response message with the linked phone number (e.g. `✅ Linked! WhatsApp is ready (+628…)`). Both behaviours are handled at the gateway handler layer (`web.login.wait`) via a new `logoutByAccountId` method on `ChannelGatewayAdapter` and a `who` field on `ChannelLoginWithQrWaitResult`.
